## Aim and Scope / 办刊宗旨与范围

The journal is dedicated to the interdisciplinary study of parapsychology, traditional Chinese culture, consciousness studies, anomalous human experiences, UAP/UFO studies, field investigation, historical archives, and related theoretical or empirical research.

本刊致力于推动超心理学、中华传统文化、意识研究、不明人体与自然现象、UAP/UFO研究、田野调查、历史档案、身心修养、易学、风水、儒释道文化及相关跨学科研究。

The journal welcomes original research articles, review articles, field reports, archival studies, scholar profiles, book reviews, academic dialogues, and theoretical essays.

## Editorial Policy / 编辑政策

The journal follows an academic editorial process and encourages responsible, evidence-based, and clearly documented research. Submitted articles should be original, properly cited, and written in accordance with academic standards.

本刊坚持学术规范，鼓励基于事实、材料、实验、田野记录和严谨论证的研究。投稿文章应具有原创性，引用规范，资料来源清楚，观点表达审慎。

---

## Peer Review / 同行评议

Submitted manuscripts may be reviewed by at least two independent reviewers or editorial experts according to the nature of the submission.

来稿原则上根据文章性质安排不少于两位独立评审人或相关领域编辑专家审阅。编辑部保留对文章进行学术规范、语言表达和格式统一处理的权利。

---

## Open Access Policy / 开放获取政策

This journal provides open access to its published content. Readers may read, download, and share published articles for academic, educational, and non-commercial purposes with proper citation.

本刊实行开放获取。读者可在注明出处的前提下，为学术、教育和非商业用途阅读、下载、引用和传播已发表文章。
